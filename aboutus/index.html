About us

We are a world changing project camp. Our camp is the ideal mix of co-living, co-working and improving yourself. Within three weeks on our gorgeous villa you will realize your idea with other carefully selected professionals and be funded if the project will be good enough presented. At the camp we are waiting for people who are willing to help other communities in that way and are interested in modern technology, programming, design, art and music. Special knowledge to participate in the camp is not required but it's appreciated if you are keen to step out of your comfort zone.

We are a platform designed to create social impact, the platform connects like-minded people so they can build a community and work towards making a positive change. What you can expect – to share an amazing experience co-living and co-working with others while you grow and learn. Over the 3 weeks you will learn more about our platform, how to use the platform and train how to become a digital volunteer. A page for the community will be set up and you will feel confident in your role as a digital volunteer and see how we operates. You will also get to see beautiful spots in Malta. When the camp is over you can also continue to work on the project. The experience is exciting, fun and has purpose. If this sounds like the project for come and join us!
