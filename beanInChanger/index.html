Become members

All of us believe that living your life decently is possible!

Join us by becoming a digital volunteer! Or simply by joining our exciting journey!
We want to contribute to helping communities change the world for the better.
We want to bring together people from various fields - design, programming, journalism
and provide them with the opportunity to contribute to the development of world-changing
communities of all different shapes and sizes.

We know that for this we need a new, independent and functional tool - this is Freenity.
We are welcome all people able to communicate and create the content for communities.
This Freenity Volunteers Camp will be dedicated to communities in Colombia.

Program

Content:

The program takes 3 weeks in Malta

The number of participants: 7

The number of facilitators: 2


Tools:

  • design thinking
  • non-formal education
  • teambuilding
  • workshop
  • stand-up
  • co-living and dinners

Plan:

1-2 days: meeting up

3-6 days: generation of ideas

7th day: day-off

8-9 days: planning

10-12 days: work on the tasks

13th day: day off

14-16 days: work on the tasks

17 day: final stage

18 day: presentation of the project

Daily schedule

7.00 - wake up

Accommodation

in Meliõhi - one of the most beautiful and

located on the cliff from where you can
overlook the seaside.

​Inside of the house there are several
bedrooms and toilets, fully equipped kitchen,
work area, wide veranda with

​Shops, cafés and beach are in walking

Detaled condition

Selection consists of 4 steps:

Submission of application

Online-interview

Confirmation of participation by inChange

Confirmation of participation by the participant(booking the flight tickets)

Basic information:

Each camp is 3 weeks long

inChange Project Camp provides accommodation (2-3 people in a room), program and tools.

The participant is obliged to have funds to support himself during the camp.

Terms of participation:

Over 18 years

Availability of schengen for a trip to Malta.

Stay for all three weeks of arrival

Conduct a workshop or a lecture for other participants
